FAQ

Will this run in my arena booth?

Yes. MicPrompt is browser‑based. Use the built‑in Control Room and route audio to your PA.

Can I clone a voice?

Yes on Announcer’s Booth (individual) and higher. Upload a reference and use it on any template.

Do you support multi‑sport?

Yes. Swap template packs (Hockey, Baseball, etc.). Your roster and sponsors stay intact.

CAD pricing?

Yep — prices can display in CAD and charge via Stripe with tax settings as needed.
