FAQ

Does MicPrompt replace our announcer?

No — it enhances them. MicPrompt handles timing, scripts and sponsor reads so announcers can focus on delivery.

Does it work without Wi‑Fi?

Yes. Preload your game and MicPrompt continues offline. All reads are logged and sync when you reconnect.

Can we use our own voice?

Yes. Choose from multiple styles or add a custom voice so your rink sounds consistent all season.
